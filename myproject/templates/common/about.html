About eVoteHub

Our Mission

eVoteHub is designed to revolutionize campus elections by providing a seamless, transparent, and digital voting experience.

How It Works

  • Students can nominate themselves for elections.
  • HoDs review and approve/reject nominations.
  • The Presiding Officer finalizes candidates and sets up online voting.
  • Students vote in two phases: Class Representatives & Higher Positions.

Why Choose eVoteHub?

eVoteHub eliminates manual paperwork, prevents fraud, and ensures a fair election process with secure online voting.
