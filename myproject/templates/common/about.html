About eVoteHub

Revolutionizing campus elections with transparency and convenience

Our Mission

eVoteHub is designed to revolutionize campus elections by providing a seamless, transparent, and secure digital voting experience. We believe that student governance should be accessible to all, with a voting process that is both efficient and trustworthy.

Our platform was built with the core values of:

  • Transparency - Every step of the election process is visible and trackable
  • Security - Advanced encryption and verification systems protect vote integrity
  • Accessibility - A user-friendly interface that works on any device
  • Fairness - Equal opportunities for all candidates with standardized processes

How It Works

1

Nominations

Students can easily nominate themselves for elections through our secure portal, submitting all required documentation digitally.

2

Verification

Department Heads review and approve/reject nominations based on eligibility criteria, with automated notifications to candidates.

3

Election Setup

The Presiding Officer finalizes the candidate list and configures the voting system with specific dates, positions, and rules.

4

Two-Phase Voting

Students participate in a structured voting process - first electing Class Representatives, followed by Higher Position elections.
