Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can we implement eVoteHub at our school?

Most implementations take 2-4 weeks from initial setup to being fully operational. Our team will work with you to ensure a smooth transition and provide training for all system administrators.

Is eVoteHub compatible with our existing student information system?

eVoteHub is designed to integrate with most major student information systems. We offer APIs and custom integration services to ensure compatibility with your existing infrastructure.

How secure is the voting process?

Security is our top priority. eVoteHub employs end-to-end encryption, secure authentication protocols, and regular security audits. Each vote is anonymous yet verifiable, ensuring both privacy and integrity.
